# Meet the Mapflow AI Agent

We are excited to introduce the **Mapflow AI Agent** — a conversational assistant that turns a one-line idea into a finished AI-mapping run.

## From description to map, in one conversation

Until now, launching a processing in Mapflow meant choosing a model, drawing an AOI, picking an imagery source, checking limits, and estimating cost — step by step. With the AI Agent, you just describe what you want.

Open your dashboard, click **Create project**, type a description like *"Create a map of all trees in London Hyde Park"*, and hit **Create project with AI Agent**. The agent takes it from there.

## What the agent can do

Behind the scenes, the agent orchestrates a focused toolset that mirrors the Mapflow API:

- **Understands your area.** It resolves place names into geometries, accepts GeoJSON, and can buffer a point or polygon into a usable AOI.
- **Picks the right model.** It checks which models your plan unlocks and recommends the one that matches your task.
- **Finds the right imagery.** It searches basemaps and commercial catalogs, filters by date, cloud cover, and resolution, and recommends the best fit.
- **Respects your plan and budget.** It checks your limits, estimates the cost in credits, and asks before spending anything.
- **Runs and monitors.** Once you confirm, it launches the processing and reports back on the status.
- **Answers questions.** It can pull from the Mapflow documentation to explain models, pricing, or workflow choices on the spot.

## Why it matters

The agent removes the busywork of configuring a processing without taking the controls away from you. Every paid action — ordering imagery, starting a run — still requires an explicit confirmation. You stay in charge; the agent handles the plumbing.

## Try it now

The Mapflow AI Agent is available today in the Mapflow web app for all users. Open [app.mapflow.ai](https://app.mapflow.ai), start a new project, and click **Create project with AI Agent**.

For the full workflow and the list of tools the agent uses, see the [Mapflow AI Agent guide](https://docs.mapflow.ai/en/latest/userguides/mapflow_agent.html).
